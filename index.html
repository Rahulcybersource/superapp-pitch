Why hasn't Healthify built this?

Legacy apps are built on Reactive Databases. Even with chatbots, they wait for you to type what you ate, then spit out a number. It is fundamentally passive. Human physiology has too many overlapping variables for hardcoded algorithms to handle.

Generative AI is dead. Agentic AI is here.

The Master Node Architecture

Three specialized AI models governed by a central intelligence, replacing every other app on your phone to create the ultimate Superapp.

Nutritional Agent

  • Vision Calorie Scanner: Point your camera at a plate. It estimates macros instantly. Zero-friction logging.
  • Cultural Fluidity: Doesn't force Western diets. Maps macro-perfect plans to your authentic regional cuisine (e.g., South Indian, Mexican).

Kinetic Agent

  • Dynamic Progression: Gym animations that visually scale from absolute beginner (bodyweight) to complex barbell biomechanics.
  • Context-Aware: "My knee hurts and I have 15 mins." The agent autonomously rewrites the entire workout on the fly.

Recovery Agent

  • Consistency Tracking: Monitors daily stress levels and erratic sleep deficits through natural language or wearable data.
  • State-Based Protocols: Generates highly specific meditation or wind-down routines based exactly on *why* you are stressed today.
